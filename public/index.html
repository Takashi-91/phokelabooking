About Phokela Guest House

Established in 2010, Phokela Guest House has been providing authentic South African hospitality for over a decade. Nestled in the heart of the beautiful Limpopo province, our guest house offers a perfect blend of traditional architecture and modern comfort.

Our mission is to create memorable experiences for every guest while showcasing the rich culture and natural beauty of South Africa. From our warm staff to our carefully designed rooms, every detail reflects our commitment to excellence.

Our Story

A journey of passion, dedication, and South African hospitality

The Beginning

Founded by the Phokela family in 2010, our guest house started as a dream to share the beauty of South African culture and hospitality with visitors from around the world.

Growth & Recognition

Over the years, we've expanded our facilities while maintaining our commitment to personalized service and authentic experiences. Our efforts have been recognized with numerous hospitality awards.

Looking Forward

Today, we continue to evolve while staying true to our roots, offering modern comfort in a setting that celebrates South African heritage and natural beauty.

Our Location

Phokela Guest House is strategically located in the heart of the Drakensberg Mountains, offering easy access to some of South Africa's most spectacular natural attractions. Our prime location provides the perfect balance of seclusion and convenience.
