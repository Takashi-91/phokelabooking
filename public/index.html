Our Story

Founded in 2010, Phokela Guest House began as a dream to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience the natural beauty of South Africa while enjoying world-class hospitality. What started as a small family-run establishment has grown into a premier destination that welcomes guests from around the world.

Our name "Phokela" means "to rest" in the local language, and that's exactly what we provide - a peaceful retreat where you can unwind, recharge, and create lasting memories. Nestled in the heart of the mountains, our guest house offers breathtaking views and a tranquil atmosphere that's perfect for both relaxation and adventure.

Today, we continue to uphold the values of family, community, and exceptional service that have been our foundation since day one. Every guest who walks through our doors becomes part of the Phokela family.

Our Location

Phokela Guest House is strategically located in the heart of the Drakensberg Mountains, offering easy access to some of South Africa's most spectacular natural attractions. Our prime location provides the perfect balance of seclusion and convenience.
